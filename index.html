Game Rules

  • Use 'WASD' or 'ARROW'(←,↑,→,↓) keys to move your player.
  • Use SPACE to shoot.
  • Shoot all the approaching aliens before they reach the bottom to advance to the next level.
  • Gain a bonus life every 10,000 points
  • Loose a life if you run into an alien or hit by an alien bullet
  • Shoot down the flying saucer for a 1,000 point bonus
  • Aliens increase in speed each level you advance.
  • Aliens begin to shoot back after you reach level 3.
